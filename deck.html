The Problem

Travelers explore blind.

😰 No context — you're standing in front of a landmark but don't know its significance, history, or what's nearby
🔄 No memory — every trip starts from zero. Apps don't remember your preferences, past visits, or what you liked
📵 No safety link — family and friends have no idea where you are or if you're safe while traveling
The Solution

Your AI travel companion that sees, remembers, and protects.

📸

Snap & Understand

Point at any landmark. AI identifies the place, explains its significance, and surfaces safety tips and nearby attractions.

🧠

Smart Context

Chat with an AI that remembers your preferences, past visits, and conversations. Gets smarter with every trip.

🛡️

Guardian Ping

Real-time safety check-ins sent to a guardian's dashboard. AI-generated location summaries via WebSockets.

What Makes It Smart

3 layers of persistent memory

⚡

Analysis Cache (KV Store)

AI scene analysis is cached per landmark — instant on revisit, saves API calls and cost

🎯

Preference Learning (KV + DB)

AI extracts preferences from chat (cuisine, safety, interests) and tailors future recommendations

💬

Conversation Memory (KV Store)

Chat context persists per landmark — resume conversations across sessions with full history

📋

Travel Plans & Visit Tracking (DB)

Daily itineraries, visit counts, photo storage — your full travel journal persisted
